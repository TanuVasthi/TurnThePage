Guide To Sell

Guide to Sell Your Book

Welcome to our Book Selling Website! Follow these steps to successfully sell your book:

  1. Fill in the details of your book in the form below. Provide accurate information about the book name, author, condition, and original price.
  2. Write a brief review of your book in the "Book Review" section.
  3. Specify the preferred payment mode by selecting either UPI or Bank Account.
  4. Click on the "Calculate Price" button to get the estimated selling price for your book.
  5. Enter your personal details and contact information in the "Your Details" section.
  6. Proceed to the "Payment Details" section by clicking the "Proceed" button.

Guide for Payment Details

Thank you for reaching the Payment Details section. Follow these steps to receive your payment:

  1. If you prefer UPI as your payment mode, provide your UPI ID in the "For receiving through UPI" section.
  2. If you prefer payment through your bank account, fill in the "For receiving in Bank Account" details, including Account Number and IFSC Code.
  3. Click the "Submit" button to finalize and submit your payment details.

Once your book is sold, we will process the payment using the details you provided. Please ensure the accuracy of the information to avoid any payment delays.

Ensure all the information provided is accurate and up-to-date to facilitate a smooth selling process. Once you've completed these steps, your book will be ready to be listed on our platform.
